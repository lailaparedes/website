about

My journey into software engineering and UX design/research has been shaped by a profound appreciation for design principles. Hailing from Los Angeles, California, my initial attraction to the world of design was sparked by a fusion of interests in puzzle solving, film, and doodling. These diverse passions converged, leading me to recognize the pivotal role that design plays in creating intuitive and engaging user experiences.

As a software engineer, I find that the marriage of functionality and aesthetics is crucial in crafting digital solutions that not only meet technical requirements but also resonate with users on a visual and interactive level. This intersection of creativity and problem-solving has become the driving force behind my pursuit of excellence in both software engineering and UX design/research.
